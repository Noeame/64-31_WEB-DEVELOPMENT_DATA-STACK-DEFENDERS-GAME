Let's Play!

  1. Register/Login: Register with your name or login to continue.
  2. Start Level: Begin with your character on a platform, facing another platform.
  3. Extend Stick: Hold the mouse to extend a stick, release to stop.
  4. Bridge Gap: Stick must be long enough to reach the next platform. Too short or too long results in failure.
  5. Cross Over: Character crosses over automatically on a successful bridge.
